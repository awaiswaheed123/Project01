Registration will automatically be considered cancelled on the expiry of security clearance. The Firm’s registration/ security clearance is to be renewed after every five years. The firm has to apply 6 – 8 months prior to expiry of its registration / security clearance.

Renewal of the firm’s registration will not be granted and its name can be removed from the list of registered firms, in following cases:-
a. Failure to quote against ITs on five successive occasions.
b. Submission of four successive quotations, which are 50% above the quotations ultimately accepted, i.e. price in 4 successive quotations remain more than 50% above the price of accepted items.
