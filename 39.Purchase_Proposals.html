Purpose
This document is prepared to seek approval of CFA for procurement. Whenever a contract/ ULPO is beyond the financial power of a procuring agency then same shall be forwarded to the next tier in the financial ladder for approval. The process document used for this purpose is referred as PP.

Processing Instructions

a. PPs will be processed on eARMS application.
b. PPs of higher value will not be split, to bring them within the purview of certain financial appointment.
