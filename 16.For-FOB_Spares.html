Definition
All brands, local/ imported (FOR / FOB), which have been cleared through testing and evaluation by ITD directorate for procurement/ use in Pakistan Army.

Description
There is a large variety of spare parts, which are being developed indigenously. Out of these selected/ well reputed brands have been tested by ITD directorate and cleared for use in army. Moreover, these also include those spares/ assemblies which have been developed through ITD establishments/ defence development agencies.
